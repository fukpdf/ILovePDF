Pro tips for the best results

1. Use high-contrast images

Images where the subject contrasts clearly with the background work best.

2. Tweak the threshold

Default 240 works for white backgrounds. Lower (200–220) handles light grey or off-white.

3. Use PNG, not JPG

Save the output as PNG to preserve transparency. JPG flattens transparency to white.

4. Compose with other images

Use the transparent PNG in JPG to PDF or any layout tool.
