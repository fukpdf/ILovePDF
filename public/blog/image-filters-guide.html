Pro tips for the best results

1. Sharpen subtly

Sharpen on already sharp images can over-enhance — use only on slightly soft inputs.

2. Combine in sequence

Apply filters one at a time across multiple runs for layered effects.

3. Crop and resize first

Get the composition right with Crop Image and Image Resize before filtering.

4. Remove background after

Apply colour filters first, then run Background Remover for clean composites.

Frequently asked questions

Can I adjust filter strength?

Filters apply at preset intensities. Custom intensity sliders are on the roadmap.

Can I combine multiple filters?

Apply one at a time across multiple runs. Stacked filters in one pass are coming.

Will my image look pixellated?

No. Sharp's pipeline preserves quality through filter operations.

Does it work on transparent PNGs?

Yes. Transparency is preserved through colour-affecting filters.
