Founder & Privacy-First Developer

Hi, I'm Muhammad Safdar

I'm a student and independent developer who built ILovePDF.cyou after noticing that most online PDF tools either upload your files to remote servers with unclear privacy policies, require expensive subscriptions, or lock core features behind paywalls. I wanted something different — a platform where privacy is the default, tools are genuinely free, and your documents are never stored longer than necessary. This project is community-focused and improves every week based on real feedback from users like you.
