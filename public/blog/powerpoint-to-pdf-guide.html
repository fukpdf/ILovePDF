Frequently asked questions

Will animations and transitions be preserved?

No — PDF is a static format. Only the static appearance of each slide is captured.

Can I include speaker notes?

The default conversion exports slides only. For notes, set the export layout to Notes Pages in PowerPoint first.

Does it support old .ppt files?

Yes, both legacy .ppt and modern .pptx formats convert.

Are embedded videos preserved?

No — video content can't be embedded in PDF. A still frame may be inserted in its place.
