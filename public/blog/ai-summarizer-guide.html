Step-by-step: how to use AI Summarizer

Pro tips for the best results

1. OCR scans first

Summarisation works on text. For image-only PDFs, run OCR PDF first.

2. Adjust length to need

Use 3–5 for quick triage, 10–15 for executive summaries, 20+ for detailed digests.

3. Combine with translation

Summarise first, then translate the shorter summary if needed.

4. Verify critical points

For high-stakes decisions, always verify the summary against the original — AI is a triage aid, not a replacement.
