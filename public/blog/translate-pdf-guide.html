Pro tips for the best results

1. OCR scanned PDFs first

Image-only PDFs need OCR PDF first to extract text for translation.

2. Verify critical content

For legal or medical documents, always have a human reviewer verify AI output.

3. Translate, then summarise

For long documents, translate first then run AI Summarizer on the result.

4. Re-export to PDF

After translation, use Word to PDF to lock the result back into PDF form.

Frequently asked questions

How accurate is AI translation?

Modern AI translation is excellent for most languages and contexts. Idiomatic and legal text may need human review.

Does it preserve formatting?

Text content is translated; layout may shift slightly because translated text rarely matches original length.

Can I translate scanned PDFs?

Run them through OCR PDF first to extract text, then translate.

Is the original PDF altered?

No — your input file is never modified. Translation produces a separate output.
